25// A simple game of Coordinate Matching Pennies, a modification of original MP
26// that has multiple Nash equilibria lying on a line parametrized with one
27// variable for the second player. He must coordinate his actions in the two
28// infosets that he has, in such a way that p+q=1 for NE, where p and q are
29// probabilities of playing Heads in top and bottom infosets respectively.
30//
31// For more information on this game (e.g. equilibrium sets, etc.) see
32// todo: arxiv link
33//
