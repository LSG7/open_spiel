Initializes an OpenSpiel `Bot` wrapping a BlueChip-compatible bot.

Args:

    bridge_uncontested_bidding, without forced actions.
  player_id: The id of the player the bot will act as, 0 = West (dealer), 1
    = East.

    `send_line`.
