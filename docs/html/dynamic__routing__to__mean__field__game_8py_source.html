14 """Mean field routing game policy used in N-playerrouting game.
15 
16 The policy class DerivedNPlayerPolicyFromMeanFieldPolicy convert a mean field
17 routing game policy to a N player routing game policy. It keep in memory the
18 mean field policy and convert a N player routing game state to a mean field
19 routing game state when calling action_probabilities. Therefore the mean field
20 policy can be used on a N player state. This makes the mean field equilibrium
21 policy (which is faster to compute) a policy that approximate well the
22 equilbrium N player policy (which is slower to compute) when N is large.
23 """
