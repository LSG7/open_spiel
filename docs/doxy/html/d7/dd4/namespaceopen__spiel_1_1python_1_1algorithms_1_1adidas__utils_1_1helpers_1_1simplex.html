Compute norm of gradient projected onto the tangent space of simplex.

*assumes context is gradient descent (not ascent)

Args:
  dist: np.array, distribution
  grad: np.array, gradient (same shape as distribution)
  eps: float, elements of dist in [eps, 1 - eps] are considered to be in the
    interior of the simplex. gradients on border of simplex
  simplex_tol: float, tolerance for checking if a point lies on the simplex,
    sum(vec) <= 1 + simplex_tol and all(vec > -simplex_tol). should be smaller

    mislabeled
Returns:

22def grad_norm(dist, grad, eps=1e-8, simplex_tol=1e-9):
23 """Compute norm of gradient projected onto the tangent space of simplex.
24
25 *assumes context is gradient descent (not ascent)
26
27 Args:
28 dist: np.array, distribution
29 grad: np.array, gradient (same shape as distribution)
30 eps: float, elements of dist in [eps, 1 - eps] are considered to be in the
31 interior of the simplex. gradients on border of simplex
32 simplex_tol: float, tolerance for checking if a point lies on the simplex,
33 sum(vec) <= 1 + simplex_tol and all(vec > -simplex_tol). should be smaller
34 than eps descent steps or points that are "leaving" simplex will be
35 mislabeled
36 Returns:
37 float, norm of projected gradient
38 """
39 if simplex_tol >= eps:
40 raise ValueError("simplex_tol should be less than eps")
41 grad_proj = project_grad(grad)
42 g_norm = np.linalg.norm(grad_proj)
43 if g_norm > 0:
44 # take a gradient descent step in the direction grad_proj with len eps
45 # to determine if the update is "leaving" the simplex
46 dist -= eps * grad_proj / g_norm
47 if not ((np.sum(dist) <= 1 + simplex_tol) and np.all(dist >= -simplex_tol)):
48 g_norm = 0.
49 return g_norm
