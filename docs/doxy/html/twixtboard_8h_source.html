153// twixt board:
154// * the board has board_size_ * board_size_ cells
155// * the x-axis (cols) points right,
156// * the y axis (rows) points up
157// * coord labels c3, f4, d2, etc. start at the upper left corner (a1)
158// * player 0, 'x', red color, plays top/bottom
159// * player 1, 'o', blue color, plays left/right
160// * positions are labeled: col letter + row number, e.g. d4
161// * moves are labeled: player label + col letter + row number, e.g. xd4
162// * empty cell code = 2
163// * corner cell code = 3
164//
165// example 8 x 8 board:
166// move: xc5, player 0 action: 19, red peg at [2,3]
167// move: of5, player 1 action: 43, blue peg at [5,3]
168// move: xd3, player 0 action: 29, red peg at [3,5]
169// link from [2,3] to [3,5]
170// cell[2][3].links = 00000001 (bit 1 set for NNE direction)
171// cell[3][5].links = 00010000 (bit 5 set for SSW direction)
172//
173// a b c d e f g h
174// 7 3| 2 2 2 2 2 2 | 3 1
175// --|------------------------|--
176// 6 2| 2 2 2 2 2 2 | 2 2
177// | |
178// 5 2| 2 2 [0] 2 2 2 | 2 3
179// | |
180// 4 2| 2 2 2 2 2 2 | 2 4
181// | |
182// 3 2| 2 [0] 2 2 [1] 1 | 2 5
183// | |
184// 2 2| 2 2 2 2 2 2 | 2 6
185// | |
186// 1 2| 2 2 2 2 2 2 | 2 7
187// --|------------------------|--
188// 0 3| 2 2 2 2 2 2 | 3 8
189// 0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
190//
191// Actions are indexed from 0 to (board_size_ ** 2) - 1
192// except the corners (0, 7, 56, 63) which are not legal actions.
193//
194// a b c d e f g h
195// 7 | 15 23 31 39 47 55 | 1
196// --|------------------------|--
197// 6 6| 14 22 30 38 46 54 |62 2
198// | |
199// 5 5| 13 21 [29] 37 45 53 |61 3
200// | |
201// 4 4| 12 20 28 36 44 52 |60 4
202// | |
203// 3 3| 11 [19] 27 35 [43] 51 |59 5
204// | |
205// 2 2| 10 18 26 34 42 50 |58 6
206// | |
207// 1 1| 9 17 25 33 41 49 |57 7
208// --|------------------------|--
209// 0 | 8 16 24 32 40 48 | 8
210// 0 1 2 3 4 5 6 7
211//
212// mapping move to action: [c,r] => c * size + r
213// xd6 == [2,3] => 2 * 8 + 3 == 19
