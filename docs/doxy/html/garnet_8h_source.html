15// Mean Field Garnet.
16//
17// This game corresponds to a garnet defined in section 5.1 of
18// "Scaling up Mean Field Games with Online Mirror Descent", Perolat & al. 2021
19// (https://arxiv.org/pdf/2103.00623.pdf)
20//
21// A garnet is a parametrized family of randomly generated Mean Field Game. One
22// can control the number of action, the number of chance actions and the
23// sparsity of the reward.
24// - The transition is randomly generated as an unnormalized uniform(0,1) over
25// the chance actions and the next state is selected uniformly over the state
26// space.
27// - The reward is parametrized by eta as r(x,a) - eta * log(mu(x)) where r(x,a)
28// is sampled uniformly over [0,1] only with probability the sparsity and 0.0
29// otherwise.
